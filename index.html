Introduction

I’m currently a Master's student at Carnegie Mellon University, majoring in Electrical and Computer Engineering. Before that, I received my Bachelor’s Degree in Automation with Excellent Graduate Honor in 2020 from Wuhan University, China.

“Talk is cheap, show me the code.” is what I believe in. Instead of abstract and obscure concepts, I find myself enjoying more on practical engineering projects. I came to Carnegie Mellon University for this purpose as well. Between Sept. to Dec. 2020, I conducted a project building the navigation software for an Autonomous Mobile Robot (AMR) at Xiamen Saimo Jesoo Technology Co., Ltd. Previously, under the supervision of Prof. Young Fei, I worked on robot perception system design and simulation in the IntelligentIRIS Lab. I also joined the WHU Intelligence Car Race Fleet, which is supervised by Prof. Xiangtao Zhuan, during my bachelor's study and I led a team to won the first and second awards in the competition.

My current interests are mostly focused on Software Development in Computer systems, Robotics, and Internet Services. I’m taking courses (16720) Computer Vision, (18661) Machine Learning and (18645) How to Write Fast Code for this semester and planning to take (TBDS) in future.

For more info, please refer to the latest CV.
